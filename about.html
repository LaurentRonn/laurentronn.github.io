About me

I'm a creative developer with a penchant for emerging technologies and game development.
Originally a voice actor, TV actor, and TV Host starring in educational projects and content for Swedish TV and radio, I followed my curiosity and passion for math and problem solving into studies in mechanical engineering at KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, only to be caught in software development at Stockholm University.

I'm particularly interested in virtual and augmented reality, nowadays known as 'Spatial Computing', education, and the beauty of chaos. (The mathematical kind of chaos).
My passion for creativity and technology pushes me to create and explore ideas, mechanics, designs, and prototypes mostly using Unity3D, Blender, and the Adobe suite.

I'm also fluent in French, Swedish, English, Java, Python, C#, Matlab, HTML5. Oh yeah, and I can cook. And draw. And of course I'm a massive Lord of the rings fan.
